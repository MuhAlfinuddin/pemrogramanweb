FAQ

What makes koi fish special?

Koi fish are admired for their elegant swimming movements, unique color patterns, and calming presence in ornamental ponds.

Are the koi fish healthy and premium quality?

Yes, every koi is carefully selected with premium quality, vibrant colors, and active movement to ensure the best appearance.

Which koi varieties are available?

We provide various popular koi types such as Kohaku, Showa, Bekko, Asagi, and many other elegant varieties.

How to care for koi fish properly?

Maintain clean water quality, provide balanced nutrition, and ensure enough pond space for healthy koi growth.

Can koi fish create a relaxing atmosphere?

Absolutely. The graceful movement and soothing colors of koi fish help create a peaceful and luxurious pond environment.
